Risk Management in Forex

Published: January 15, 2026

The 1-2% Rule

Never risk more than 1-2% of your total trading account on a single trade. This ensures you can survive a series of losses.

Stop-Loss Orders

Always set a stop-loss before entering a trade. This defines your maximum loss and removes emotion from trading.

Risk-Reward Ratio

Only take trades with a minimum 1:2 risk-reward ratio. For every $1 you risk, aim to make at least $2.

Diversification

Avoid overexposure to a single currency pair. Spread your risk across different markets and timeframes.
